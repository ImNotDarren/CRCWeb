Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 8, 2026

CRCWeb is a research platform developed by Emory University to support research studies involving individuals affected by colorectal cancer. Protecting your privacy and personal information is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected through CRCWeb, how it is used, how it is protected, and your rights as a participant.

Information We Collect

CRCWeb may collect the following types of information when you use the platform:

  • Account information, such as name, email address, or study ID.
  • Patient-reported outcomes, including survey responses related to symptoms, quality of life, behaviors, or experiences.
  • Demographic information, such as age, gender, race/ethnicity, or education, if required by the research study.
  • Usage data, such as login activity or survey completion status.

Note: CRCWeb does not collect financial information, contact lists, call history, or unrelated personal data.

How Your Information Is Used

Information collected through CRCWeb is used solely for research purposes, including:

  • Conducting approved research studies.
  • Monitoring study participation and data completeness.
  • Analyzing aggregated data to improve understanding of health outcomes.
  • Improving the design and usability of CRCWeb.

Your information will not be used for marketing or commercial purposes.

Data Storage and Security

  • Data entered into CRCWeb are stored on secure, password-protected servers managed by Emory University or its approved vendors.
  • Data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard security protocols (e.g., HTTPS/TLS).
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized study personnel who have completed required research and privacy training.

Data Sharing

Your information will only be shared with authorized personnel involved in the study. We do not sell or trade your personal data to third parties.
